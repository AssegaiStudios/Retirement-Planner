# NHS Retirement Planner with Cash Streams

An Excel-based retirement planning workbook created by **Assegai Studios**, designed with NHS staff and NHS Pension Scheme members in mind.

This tool helps users model retirement income, savings, pension benefits, cash streams, and possible funding gaps across different retirement scenarios.

## Features

- NHS Pension-focused retirement modelling
- Retirement age scenario comparison
- State Pension income assumptions
- Private pension and investment projections
- ISA, cash savings, and other capital stream modelling
- One-off future cash injections, such as inheritance or planned lump sums
- Inflation-adjusted retirement income planning
- Estimated income shortfall or surplus
- Estimated additional lump sum required today
- Estimated additional monthly saving required
- Dashboard summary for quick comparison

## Who this is for

This planner is intended for:

- NHS employees
- NHS Pension Scheme members
- People planning retirement income needs
- Individuals who want to compare retirement age scenarios
- Users who want a transparent spreadsheet-based planning tool

## What is included

The workbook includes input sheets, projection models, cash stream tracking, and dashboard outputs. Users can adjust assumptions to explore different retirement paths.
