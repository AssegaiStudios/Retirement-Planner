# Security and Privacy

This repository contains an Excel workbook for retirement planning. Users should not upload or share completed versions of the workbook containing personal financial information.

## Sensitive information

Do not include any of the following in issues, pull requests, screenshots, or shared workbooks:

- National Insurance numbers
- NHS Pension member numbers
- Salary details
- Pension statements
- Bank details
- Investment account details
- Personal addresses
- Dates of birth
- Any other private financial or identifying information

## Reporting a concern

If you find a privacy, security, or data exposure issue, please contact Assegai Studios through the repository owner or official contact channel rather than posting sensitive details publicly.
